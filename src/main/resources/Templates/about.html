F1SIM

About

  • What is F1SIM?

    F1SIM is a lightweight Formula 1 race simulator that uses real racing concepts such as tyre wear, race pace, pit strategies, weather, and driver performance to simulate full race results.

    The goal is to provide realistic-feeling race outcomes without relying on heavy graphics or complex physics engines.

  • How it works

    Every lap is simulated individually using:
    Driver performance ratings,
    Tyre compound behaviour,
    Tyre wear progression,
    Track characteristics,
    Weather conditions

    The system calculates lap times, decides pit stops, applies degradation, updates positions, and determines race results in real time.
